🌐 About Engineering Careers

Why Engineering is India's Best Career Choice

Engineering remains the most sought-after career in India, offering strong salaries, job security, and global opportunities across industries.

📈
Growing Demand

India produces over 15 lakh engineering graduates yearly. With India's $5 trillion economy goal, demand for engineers across all sectors is at an all-time high.

💰
Strong Salaries

Fresher engineers earn ₹3–8 LPA on average. With 5+ years experience, salaries jump to ₹15–40+ LPA. Top tech engineers can earn ₹50–100+ LPA.

🌍
Global Opportunities

Indian engineers work across the USA, UK, UAE, Canada, Germany, and Australia. GATE score opens PSU jobs. Visa-ready skills are in high demand globally.

🏗️
Core to Advanced

From building bridges to designing AI chips — engineering covers everything. Whether you're interested in hands-on work or digital innovation, there's a branch for you.

📋 EAPCET Counselling

Documents Required for EAPCET Counselling

Keep these documents ready before attending TS / AP EAPCET Web Counselling. Missing even one can delay your seat allotment.

Counselling Flow
  1. Pay Fee
  2. Slot Booking
  3. Certificate Verification
  4. Candidate Registration
  5. Option Entry
  6. Print Saved Options
  7. Logout Properly
🎓
Qualifying Exam Marks Memo
Intermediate (MPC) or Diploma final year marks memo. Original + 2 photocopies required.
📝
EAPCET Hall Ticket & Rank Card
Your EAPCET 2024 hall ticket and rank card downloaded from the official website.
🏠
Residence / Domicile Certificate
Study certificate for 7 years in TS/AP OR residence certificate issued by MRO/Tahsildar for local area reservation.
📜
Caste / Category Certificate
OC / BC / SC / ST certificate issued by MRO. Required for category-based reservations and fee reimbursement.
💰
Income Certificate
Family income certificate issued by MRO/Tahsildar. Mandatory for fee reimbursement (BC/SC/ST). Valid for current year.
🆔
Aadhaar Card
Student's Aadhaar card (original + photocopy). Linked mobile number needed for OTP verification during online counselling.
📸
Passport Size Photographs
6–8 recent passport size photographs with white background. Same photo used for application must be carried.
🏫
10th (SSC) Marks Memo
SSC / 10th class marks memo as proof of date of birth. Original + photocopies required.
🏦
Bank Passbook / Account Details
Student's bank account passbook (first page with IFSC, account number). Required for fee reimbursement credit.
📱
Transfer Certificate (TC)
TC from your previous institution (Intermediate / Diploma college). Must be submitted at the time of admission.
♿
PH Certificate (if applicable)
Physically Handicapped certificate from District Medical Board for students claiming PH category reservation.
