About Mr. Salman Ahmed

Prof. Salman Ahmed is a well-known name in the field of education in Telangana. He did his Diploma from The Government Polytechnic and went on to pursue his Graduation & Masters from Osmania University.

Prof. Ahmed's journey in the teaching profession began in the year 2015 when he established Ahmed's Institute of Excellence in Hyderabad. In a short period, the institute has become a well-known name in the city. He has motivated and guided thousands of graduates for various competitive exams and has been instrumental in producing top rankers. He is known for his lectures which are very inspiring and motivating. He has helped many students to achieve their dreams of becoming successful in their careers.
